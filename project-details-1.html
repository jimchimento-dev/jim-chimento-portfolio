Project Information

  • Project Name: Banyan Hill / Money & Markets Mobile App
  • Technologies Used: React Native, JavaScript, GraphQL, Expo, NativeBase

What are the Banyan Hill and Money & Markets Mobile Apps?

The Banyan Hill and Money & Markets apps are two enterprise mobile applications that I had the pleasure of working on.

These apps serve as a one-stop-shop for our subscribers where they can login and receive up to date articles/trade alerts from the services that they are subscribed to.

I gained great experience working with React Native, Expo and GraphQL. NativeBase was used as a component library to assist in building new features.
